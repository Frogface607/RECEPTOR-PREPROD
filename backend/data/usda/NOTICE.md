# USDA FoodData Central Database

**Source:** U.S. Department of Agriculture, FoodData Central  
**Website:** https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/  
**Data License:** Public Domain  
**Last Updated:** January 18, 2025

## About this data

This dataset contains a development subset (~400 products) of nutrition information from USDA FoodData Central, selected to cover common Russian restaurant ingredients:

- Meat & Poultry
- Fish & Seafood 
- Vegetables
- Grains & Cereals
- Dairy & Eggs
- Oils & Fats
- Sauces & Condiments

The data includes per-100g nutrition values (calories, protein, fat, carbohydrates) and portion weights where applicable.

## Data Schema

- **foods**: Basic food information (fdc_id, description)
- **food_nutrient**: Nutrition values by nutrient_id 
- **food_portion**: Portion descriptions and gram weights
