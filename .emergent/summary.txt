<product_requirements>
The Receptor Pro application is designed to automate recipe card (tech card) creation and cost management for restaurateurs using AI. The core problem it solves is streamlining the tedious process of manual recipe and cost calculation.

Key features include:
- AI-generated tech cards (ingredients, steps, yield, KBJU, allergens, sales script, food pairing, photo tips).
- Automatic cost calculation based on AI's knowledge of 2025 Russian market prices, including inflation, regional coefficients, and a 3x selling price markup.
- A professional UI/UX: graphite/purple/lavender color scheme, Montserrat font, enlarged inputs, and absence of excessive emojis.
- Interactive AI/manual editing of tech cards, specifically an in-line ingredient editing table with real-time cost recalculation and a Save button.
- PDF export of tech cards.
- User management and tiered subscription system (Free, Starter, PRO, Business), with PRO features like kitchen equipment adaptation, sales script, food pairing, and photo tips.
- Voice input for dish generation.
- History of generated tech cards.

Recent implementations focused on:
- Stabilizing the interactive ingredient editor to ensure single, functional display with correct calculations and portion yield.
- Refactoring the frontend display of tech cards for clarity and a professional appearance.
- Enhancing AI prompts for accurate portion sizes, detailed Заготовки и хранение, and dynamic, market-aware price calculation.
- Implementing dedicated modal windows for PRO AI features (Sales Script, Food Pairing, Photo Tips).
- Adding animated, informative loading screens.
- Preparing a Beta version with simplified access for testing.
</product_requirements>
