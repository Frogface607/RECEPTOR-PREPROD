It offers AI-generated tech cards with detailed components, real-time cost calculation (using 2025 Russian market data), interactive editing, and clean PDF export. PRO features include Inspiration, Finances PRO, Improve Dish, Laboratory (with DALL-E 3), and Venue Customization for personalized outputs. The product vision evolved to an AI-Menu-Designer, aiming to generate entire, optimized menus based on detailed venue profiles.
