<product_requirements>
Receptor Pro is an AI-powered platform for the HoReCa industry, initially designed for automated tech card creation and cost management. It provides AI-generated tech cards with detailed components, real-time cost calculation (using 2025 Russian market data), interactive editing, and PDF export. PRO features include Inspiration, Finances PRO, Improve Dish, Laboratory (with DALL-E 3), and Venue Customization for personalized outputs. The application includes dynamic loading screens and tooltips for improved UX.

The product vision evolved to an AI-Menu-Designer (МЕНЮ ЗА 15 МИНУТ), aiming to generate entire, optimized menus based on detailed venue profiles, reducing menu development time and cost. The initial implementation featured a 5-step menu creation wizard and mass tech card generation for generated menus. User feedback highlighted the wizard's complexity, leading to a revised requirement: centralize all venue-specific and audience details into a dedicated, detailed Venue Profile. The menu creation process was to be radically simplified to just selecting a menu type (Full/Seasonal/Business Lunch) and providing free-text expectations, along with a simple dish quantity slider.
</product_requirements>
