# Pre-Demo Cleanup Checklist

Date: 2026-06-11

## Principle

Before showing Receptor to a serious operator, remove anything that makes it feel
like a generic AI toy.

Do not add new features unless they directly improve the controlled demo route.

## Demo Route

Use this route only:

1. Explain Receptor as AI operating system above iiko.
2. Venue profile / deep research.
3. iiko connection and pilot key ask.
4. Dashboard as cockpit shape.
5. Copilot as the main intelligence layer.
6. Tech Card Studio as proof of operating workflows.

## Must Fix Or Avoid

### Hero / Marketing

- Avoid fake-specific dish names in the main hero.
- Avoid making the website the center of the meeting.
- Use serious language: system, visibility, decisions, human factor, iiko layer.

### Deep Research

- If it does not show sources, call result "profile draft".
- Separate fact, assumption, and owner-provided context.
- Do not claim it "found reviews" unless it actually did.

### Dashboard

- Label demo data clearly.
- Do not pretend mock data is real.
- Use dashboard to explain future live value, not to fake a live pilot.

### Tools

- Do not open the tools grid as the main product.
- Do not say "27 prompts".
- If opening tools, go straight to Tech Card Studio.

### Copilot

- Ask business questions, not novelty questions.
- It must answer in terms of restaurant decisions.
- If it gives generic advice, do not linger.

## Good Demo Sentences

- "iiko records operations. Receptor explains them."
- "This is not a replacement for your back office. It is the AI layer above it."
- "The goal is to reduce human factor and give the brand chef visibility."
- "The system must know the restaurant before it gives advice."
- "Today I want one pilot, not a big rollout."

## Bad Demo Sentences

- "Here are 27 AI tools."
- "This is mock, but imagine..."
- "We built this overnight."
- "It can do anything."
- "It's like ChatGPT for restaurants."

## Go / No-Go

Go today only if:

- the tablet flow works;
- deep research/profile screen looks honest;
- dashboard can be framed clearly;
- Copilot gives at least one useful restaurant answer;
- you can explain the iiko key ask calmly.

If not, postpone:

> I want to bring you the pilot properly, not show a half-clean prototype. Let's
> meet tomorrow and I will bring the future in a cleaner form.
